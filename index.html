Coconut Rice Recipe

Preparation Time

It takes minimum of 45 minuites to prepare coconut rice

Ingredients

  • Long grain Rice
  • 1 Onion chopped
  • 2 cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 2 Tablespoon of Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Scotch bonnet pepper (optional)
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
  • 1 can of coconut milk (400ml)
  • Water
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of blackpepper
  • Crayfish, Fish, Chopped beef, hot dog, meat

Instructions

  1. Boil the rice in water for about 10-15 minutes (enough were it's not
    too soft -- because we will be cooking it some more).

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a cooking pot.

  3. Add the onions and stir with black pepper for one minute.

  4. Diced tomatoes, habaneros, and coconut milk with the sea salt and
    cover to cook for about 7 minutes (or when the mixture comes to a
    boil).

  5. Add 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro, 1 tablespoon of curry powder,
    1 teaspoon of black pepper, 1 teaspoon of thyme

  6. Add the rice and stir and cover for about 7 minutes (or when the
    rice is almost dry).

  7. Add the green peppers, Crayfish, Fish, Chopped beef, hot dog,
    chicken stock and let simmer until the rice has absorbed all the
    juices
